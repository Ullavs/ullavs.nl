Who I am

Front-end developer & medical doctor based in Amsterdam

I am Ulla and I am a junior front-end developer living in Amsterdam. I have multiple years of working experience as a medical doctor. However, as an advocate of 'following your heart', in 2021 I decided to change my career. I came upon coding and its community; this attracted me tremendously, so I started to learn it right away. Since then I have been studying front-end development through different online courses, while also working on the COVID-19 vaccination campaign to contribute to society.

People describe me as a reliable, precise and critical team player. I would say I am eager to learn, enthusiastic and sincere. In my free time I love to play with my dog Izzy, speed cubing, watching Netflix, playing video and board games, drinking cocktails, listening to podcasts, playing tennis, watching all kind of sports and travelling!

In October I will be starting my first job as a junior front-end developer; I am beyond excited. My goal is to finish my Scrimba and LOI study beforehand. You can check my projects below.
